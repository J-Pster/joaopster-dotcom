Every blog starts with a post like this one. I will keep it short.

I am João, a backend developer working mostly with TypeScript, Node.js, and AWS. I have been working with AI for a good while, not as a product, but as a daily work tool. I have opinions about how to use it right, and about how *not* to use it.

## Why write

Because I pile up notes, internal threads, and architecture decisions that end up buried in private repos or in my own head. I have helped entire teams with setups I learned the hard way. It makes sense to put that somewhere people can actually reach.

This will not be a step-by-step tutorial blog. It will be more about technical opinions, real project decisions, and things I learned by getting them wrong first.

## What to expect

Posts about:

- **AI applied for real**: setup, tools, how I fold it into my day without becoming a hostage to the hype
- **Backend and infrastructure**: patterns that work, and the ones that promise but do not deliver
- **Open source**: things I have built and why they exist

No promises on frequency. I post when I have something worth posting.
